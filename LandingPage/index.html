    Some of the dream destinations in India that you can explore with Indigo are:
    Ladakh: A land of high passes, snow-capped mountains, and serene lakes. Ladakh is a paradise for adventure seekers and nature lovers.
    Goa: A state of sun, sand, and sea. Goa is a popular destination for beach lovers, party goers, and culture enthusiasts. You can enjoy the vibrant nightlife, water sports, heritage sites, and delicious cuisine.
    Andaman: A group of islands in the Bay of Bengal. Andaman is a dream destination for those who love tropical beauty, coral reefs, and marine life. You can indulge in scuba diving, snorkeling, island hopping, and wildlife watching.
